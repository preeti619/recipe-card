Chocolate Smoothie

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Serving Size: 2 servings

  • 1 cup Chocolate
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes
  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
